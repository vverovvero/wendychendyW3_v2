ABOUT ME

I am a developer and artist, seeking creative challenges.

Hi, my name is Wendy. I love art. I love coding. I am passionate about how computer science and visual art can be combined, because at their core, they are both about creation and problem solving.

I am expecting to graduate from Yale University with a B.S. in Computer Science in May 2017. I have worked as an Art & Production Intern at Walt Disney Animation Studios and as a Production Engineering Intern at Blue Sky Studios.

Being able to combine a technical understanding with an artistic eye, I am ready to create something we've never seen before. The last limit is just my imagination. I want to shape the world we see because so much of our experience is rooted in vision, and by shaping what we see, I can all the more shape what we imagine, think, and feel.
